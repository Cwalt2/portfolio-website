The Cyberforce Competition is a yearly competition when the top cybersecurity students around the US compete to defend CRITICAL energy infrastructure. I attended Cyberforce 2025 and this post will describe my experience and what I learned!

Key Achievement: Auditing and overhauling a energy company website for employees and clients.

The Challenge

  • Audit and document steps to defend a compromised enterprise ecosystem
  • Present critical issues to a C Suite of executives and owners
  • Prepare infrastructure before the attack phase of the competition
  • Defend infrastructure from red team industry professionals

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Audit the company website
  • Fix any errors or exploits
  • Restore the website to a fully functional state
